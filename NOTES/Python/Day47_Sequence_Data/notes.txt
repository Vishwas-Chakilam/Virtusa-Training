# Day 47: Sequence Data Types

## Lists
A List is a mutable sequence used to store collections of data.
- Initialized with `[]`.
- `append(x)`, `insert(i, x)`, `remove(x)`, `pop([i])`.

## Tuples
A Tuple is an immutable sequence. Faster than lists safely protecting data.
- Initialized with `()`.

## Strings and Slicing
Strings are immutable arrays of characters.
- Extensively supports list slicing `[start:stop:step]`.
- Important methods: `.upper()`, `.lower()`, `.strip()`, `.split()`, `.replace()`.
