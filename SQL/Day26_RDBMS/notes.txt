Day 26: RDBMS Basics
    
    RDBMS organizes data into tables (relations), rows (tuples), cols (attributes).
    
    ACID:
    - Atomicity: all or nothing transactions.
    - Consistency: valid state before and after.
    - Isolation: concurrent trans don't interfere.
    - Durability: committed data is saved even on crash.
    
    Keys: Primary Key (unique + not null), Foreign Key (links tables), Unique Key.
